Hi, welcome to my website!

I am a Computer Science undergrad in my final semester at the Federal University of Agriculture Abeokuta, Nigeria. I am an independent researcher with my strongest research affiliation being ML Collective, where I am primarily mentored by Rosanne Liu and Jason Yosinski. My research interests are centered around tackling efficiency problems in machine learning from algorithms, methods, data, and system perspectives. In my head, the big goal is to drive accessibility and participation in the creation of the AI technologies we use so that resources cease to be a bottleneck or criteria to being part of the decision making process.

On the side, I am flexing my engineering muscles building a funded, open-source project with my friends (and my sister) called ASAlytics - a dynamic social media opinion mining platform for digital assets with real-time analytics and data retrieval from Twitter, Reddit, and GitHub.

Non-work-wise, I routinely weightlift, make music, and add to my bank of "useless" knowledge via diverse reading. Moreover, I try to finetune my clowning game with every chance I get - through my conversations with friends, via my speaking engagements, and in my head.
