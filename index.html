Works

I am a gradute of the University of Ilorin in the Department of Materials and Metallurgical Engineering , advised by Professor I. I. Ahmed.

My interests focus on tackling efficiency problems in machine learning from systems, algorithms, and data perspectives. In my head, the big goal is to drive accessibility and participation in the creation of the AI technologies we use so that resources cease to be a bottleneck or criteria for being part of the decision-making process.
