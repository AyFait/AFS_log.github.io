Hi, welcome to my website! :)

I am an incoming PhD student at the Language Technologies Institute in the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University . Before that, I completed my BSc in Computer Science at the Federal University of Agriculture Abeokuta, Nigeria, advised by Dr. Adebayo Abayomi-Alli. Since early 2021, I have been an independent researcher with ML Collective (and cameo appearances at Masakhane and Cohere For AI), where Rosanne Liu and Jason Yosinski primarily mentor me.

My research interests center around tackling efficiency problems in machine learning from algorithms, methods, data, and system perspectives. In my head, the big goal is to drive accessibility and participation in the creation of the AI technologies we use so that resources cease to be a bottleneck or criteria to being part of the decision-making process.

Non-work-wise, I routinely weightlift, make music, and add to my bank of "useless" knowledge by reading diversely. Moreover, I try to finetune my clowning game with every chance I get - through my conversations with friends, via my speaking engagements, and in my head.
